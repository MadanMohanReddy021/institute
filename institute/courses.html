Our Programs

Diploma in Computer Engineering

This course provides the students with the skills and knowledge required to work with computer systems, programming, networking, and information technology.

Diploma in Electronics and Communications Engineering

This program focuses on the design, development, and application of electronic systems and communication technologies.

Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering

The course includes the study of electrical circuits, electronic devices, and power systems to equip students with fundamental electrical engineering skills.

Diploma in Civil Engineering

This course covers the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure, such as bridges, roads, buildings, and water systems.

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

The course provides knowledge in mechanics, thermodynamics, material science, and the design and production of mechanical systems.

Diploma in Mining Engineering

This program focuses on the extraction of minerals from the Earth, the planning and operation of mines, and the development of mining techniques.
